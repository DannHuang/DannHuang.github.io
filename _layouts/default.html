Jen-Yuan (Joy) Huang

Hello! I am a graduate student at the School of Intelligence Science and Technology at Peking University, advised by Professor Tong Lin. My research interest lies in computer vision and generative models. I enjoy developing fancy AI-generated content (AIGC) models, and I am currently a member of InstantX Team.

I received my Bachelor's degree in Industrial Engineering from Nanjing University, with a specialization in optimization and operation research. During my undergraduate studies, I had the opportunity to work closely with Professor Xiaopeng Luo.
